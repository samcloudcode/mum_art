# Art Inventory UI/UX Design

## Design Philosophy

**User**: An artist managing physical prints across home and multiple galleries.

**Core Principle**: "Where are my prints, and have I been paid?"

The interface should feel like a personal assistant, not a database. Every screen answers a real question the artist might ask.
